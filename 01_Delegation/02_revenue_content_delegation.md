* **What Success Looks Like:** A structural, data-rich comparison table, a high-converting landing page structure, and two automated email copy templates optimized to turn a technical reader into a free-trial user.

### Step 3: Task Breakdown & The Holistic 4D Delegation Plan

To execute this holistically using the **Anthropic 4D AI Fluency Framework**, we break down the execution into precise human and machine roles.

```
  [1. Human Intent & Tech Specs] ──> [2. AI Structural Layout] ──> [3. Human High-Value Injections]
                                                                             │
  [5. Premium Portfolio Asset] <── [4. AI Conversion Formatting Pass] <──────┘

```

#### Task 1: Competitor Matrix & Value Proposition Data

* **Human Role (Domain Expert):** Outlining the specific tech specs that engineers care about (e.g., open-source vs. fully managed, latency, cost metrics). You provide the truth.
* **AI Role (The Analyst):** Instantly synthesizing the raw technical feature sheets into a clean, LLM-friendly comparative Markdown table.
* **4D Lens (Delegation):** Collaborative. You dictate the critical differentiators; the AI structures the data beautifully.

#### Task 2: High-Converting Copy Drafting (The Core Landing Page)

* **Human Role (The Copywriter):** Drafting the main hook, the customer pain point, and the primary Call to Action (CTA).
* **AI Role (The Expansion Engine):** Writing the supporting explanatory feature paragraphs around your hooks, ensuring sentences stay crisp (under 22 words) and free of cliché AI buzzwords like *"revolutionize"* or *"testament"*.
* **4D Lens (Description):** You give the AI a strict persona prompt: *"Act as an elite B2B copywriter. Avoid fluff. Write to senior developers using direct, concise sentences."*

#### Task 3: Lead Nurturing Email Sequence (Email 1 & Email 2)

* **Human Role (The Auditor):** Ensuring the emotional angle targets developer frustrations (e.g., hidden cloud hosting costs, system crashes).
* **AI Role (The Layout Writer):** Writing the physical structural template blocks for the email, including high-click subject line variations.
* **4D Lens (Discernment & Diligence):** You audit every single sentence to ensure the tone sounds authoritative, helpful, and completely human before committing it to your portfolio repository.
